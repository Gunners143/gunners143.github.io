What this clock does

This online clock shows the current time in your browser and updates automatically. You can switch between 12-hour and 24-hour format, and view today’s date and your local timezone.

How to use

  • Use the format button to switch between 12-hour and 24-hour time.
  • The time updates automatically every second.
  • Your format preference can be saved on your device (if enabled).

Common uses

  • Time displays in large rooms
  • Large display for the visually impaired

Tip: If you are comparing times across regions, check the timezone display.
