Privacy Policy

Overview

This website is designed to work without collecting personal information. You can use the clock, timer and stopwatch without creating an account.

Data we collect

We do not collect or store personal data on our servers.

Cookies

This site does not set cookies for its core functionality.

If we use Google AdSense or similar providers, those providers may use cookies or similar technologies to measure ad performance and/or show relevant ads. If and when advertising is enabled, this policy will be updated to explain what technologies are used and how you can manage your choices.

Contact
