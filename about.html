About

Clocks & Timers is a simple set of tools you can use instantly — a clock, a countdown timer, and a stopwatch. The goal is to keep things fast, clear, and distraction-free.

Design principles

  • Simple: the tool is the focus.
  • Fast: minimal loading and no unnecessary features.
  • Mobile-friendly: designed for one-hand use where possible.

How the site is supported

This site may show advertising in the future to help cover hosting and development time. If ads are enabled, they’ll be placed in a predictable area of the page to avoid disrupting use of the tools.
